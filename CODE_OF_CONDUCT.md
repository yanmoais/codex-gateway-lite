# Code of Conduct

Codex Gateway Lite welcomes constructive, technical, and respectful collaboration.

Please:

- Be kind and specific when discussing bugs or design tradeoffs.
- Keep reports focused on reproducible facts.
- Do not publish other people's secrets, API keys, logs, private paths, or personal data.
- Do not harass, threaten, or deliberately derail maintainers or contributors.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate these expectations.
